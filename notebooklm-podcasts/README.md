# NotebookLM Podcast Packets

These files are source packets for turning the 90 cosplay wig lessons into short NotebookLM Audio Overviews.

Recommended use:
- Upload one episode file into a NotebookLM notebook.
- Generate a short Audio Overview from that source.
- Standard NotebookLM accounts may be limited to 3 Audio Overviews per day, so plan on about four days for all 10 episodes.
- Listen back before relying on an episode, especially for heat, adhesive, dye, blade, dust, or skin-contact topics.

Episode list:
- Episode 01: Wig Anatomy And Reference Views - lessons 1-9
- Episode 02: Setup, Fit, And Heat Safety - lessons 10-18
- Episode 03: Steam, Care, And Cutting Setup - lessons 19-27
- Episode 04: Cutting Shape, Layers, And Volume - lessons 28-36
- Episode 05: Texture, Product, And Adhesives - lessons 37-45
- Episode 06: Anime Hair Shapes And Spikes - lessons 46-54
- Episode 07: Wefts, Ponytails, Braids, And Edges - lessons 55-63
- Episode 08: Hairlines, Ventilating, And Color Tests - lessons 64-72
- Episode 09: Color Choices And Structural Builds - lessons 73-81
- Episode 10: Transport, Samples, Photos, And Review - lessons 82-90
